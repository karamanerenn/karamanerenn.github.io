Data Center Waste Heat Recovery

Role: Thermal system designer — Tech: LMTD method, plate heat exchanger, SolidWorks, thermal analysis

Overview

This project focuses on recovering waste heat generated in data centers using a counter-flow plate heat exchanger system.

My Contributions

  • Heat transfer calculations (LMTD)
  • Plate heat exchanger selection
  • Reynolds & Nusselt numbers for turbulent flow
  • System layout in SolidWorks

Results

~78% energy recovery; suitable for space heating or hot water, reducing operational costs.
