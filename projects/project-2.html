Data Center Waste Heat Recovery

Role: Thermal system designer — Tech: LMTD method, plate heat exchanger, SolidWorks, thermal analysis

Overview

This project focuses on recovering waste heat generated in data centers using a counter-flow plate heat exchanger system. By redirecting and utilizing this heat, overall energy efficiency was significantly improved.

My Contributions

  • Performed heat transfer calculations using the LMTD method
  • Selected a plate heat exchanger with optimal surface area
  • Calculated Reynolds and Nusselt numbers for turbulent flow
  • Designed the system layout in SolidWorks

Results

Achieved approximately 78% energy recovery efficiency. The system can be applied for office heating or hot water supply, reducing overall operational costs.
