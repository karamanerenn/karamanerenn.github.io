AI-Assisted Smart Bulletproof Vest

Role: Lead student researcher | Tech: Arduino, Python, conductive textiles

Overview

Conductive sensors integrated into the vest form a grid that detects impact coordinates. The system sends signals for visualization and alerting.

My Contributions

  • Sensor matrix design and wiring layout
  • Arduino firmware and data acquisition
  • Python scripts for real-time plotting

Results

Accurate impact localization within expected tolerance; modular design for future upgrades.
